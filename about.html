About Kampus Mart

Kampus Mart is a dedicated marketplace app designed for students and campus communities. Our mission is to make buying and selling on campus easy, safe, and fun.

  • Browse & Search: Find textbooks, electronics, clothing, dorm essentials, and more.
  • Interactive Product Pages: See detailed info, adjust quantity, rate, and comment on products.
  • Easy Cart Management: Add, update, or remove items before checkout.
  • Seller Tools: Track your sales and manage your listings with ease.
  • Personalized Experience: Choose your interests and get tailored suggestions.
  • Safe & Secure: Connect with verified campus members for peace of mind.

Whether you're looking to buy, sell, or simply explore what your campus community has to offer, Kampus Mart is your go-to platform. Join us and make campus life more connected and convenient!
